The Challenge in Indian Agriculture

Supply Chain Fragmentation

India's agricultural supply chain involves multiple intermediaries, causing price inflation and reducing farmer income.

Limited Traceability

Consumers cannot verify the origin of products or authenticate their quality and farming practices.

Inefficient Payments

Delayed payments and financial inclusion barriers affect farmer livelihoods and operational sustainability.

Post-Harvest Loss

India loses 40% of produce due to inefficient storage, transportation, and distribution systems.

How AgroTrace Solves These Problems

Immutable Traceability

End-to-end tracking with tamper-proof blockchain records from farm to table

QR Verification

Simple QR scanning to verify authenticity and view complete product journey

Direct Payments

Secure, instant crypto payments to farmers eliminating intermediaries

IoT Integration

Real-time monitoring of storage conditions and transportation

Smart Certifications

Automated verification of organic, fair trade, and other certifications

Market Analytics

Data-driven insights for better pricing and demand forecasting
